Safety is our priority

We uphold the highest standards. All childcare providers undergo a 6-step safety verification to give you peace of mind.

  1. Vulnerable Record Check
  2. Criminal Record Check
  3. Social Media Screening
  4. CPR + First Aid Certification
  5. Personal Reference Check
  6. Virtual interview + ID Verification
